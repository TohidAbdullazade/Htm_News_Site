UEFA made a statement about the new format of the Champions League from the next season

Union of European Football Associations (UEFA) Champions from next season He presented pictures to explain the new format of his league. So that, From the 2024/2025 season, there will be 36 teams instead of 32 as before in the Champions League will perform. Previously, clubs were divided into groups of four teams each If it was divided, now all 36 teams in the Champions League are united using the so-called Swiss system in the tournament table will be done. According to the new format, eight rounds in the group stage (was six), the team will play four games at home and another four games away.
